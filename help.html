Help & FAQ 🤖
Getting Started
How do I create an account?
Open newstally.online → Tap Profile icon → Sign in with Google or Email → Choose a unique username → Done! Your account is ready.
What are the username rules?
Username must: be 3-30 characters long, contain only letters (a-z), numbers (0-9), dots (.) or underscores (_), and be unique. No two users can have the same username.
How do I change my profile photo?
Profile photo comes from your Google account automatically. To change it: update your Google profile photo → it will reflect on NewsTally automatically after re-login.
How to install NewsTally as an app?
Android: Open newstally.online in Chrome → Menu (3 dots) → "Add to Home Screen". iPhone: Open in Safari → Share button → "Add to Home Screen". The app icon will appear on your home screen!
Socialgati — Posts & Social
How do I create a post?
Tap the ✏️ pen button at the bottom center → Write your text (max 500 chars) → Add hashtags with # → Add image if needed → Tap "Share". Your post appears in everyone's feed!
How do I repost news to Socialgati?
Open any news article → Tap the ↺ Repost button → News gets shared to Socialgati feed. Note: Same news can only be reposted once per user.
What is "Gati"?
Gati is NewsTally's version of "Like"! Tap the ❤️ heart button to Gati a post. You can also double-tap any post for a quick Gati.
How to follow someone?
Tap on any user's profile photo or username → Their profile opens → Tap the "Follow" button (purple). You'll start seeing their posts in your feed.
News & Search
How to search for news?
On the News page, tap the 🔍 search button (bottom-left floating button) → Type your topic, keyword or source name → Live results appear → Tap any result to read the full article.
How to save news articles?
Open any news article → Tap the 🔖 bookmark icon in the header or source bar → Article is saved. View saved articles: Profile → Saved tab (🔖 icon).
Can I listen to news articles?
Yes! Open any news article → Tap the 🔊 speaker icon → The article will be read aloud using text-to-speech. Tap again to stop.
Account & Settings
How to enable Dark Mode?
Go to Profile → Tap ☰ Settings icon → Toggle "Dark Mode" on. You can also set "Auto Dark Schedule" to automatically switch at specific times.
How to delete my account?
Profile → Settings → Scroll down → "Delete Account" → Confirm. Warning: This is permanent and all your data will be deleted.
Why am I not getting notifications?
Make sure you're signed in. Also check: browser notification permissions are allowed for newstally.online. You receive notifications when someone likes, comments or follows you.
Something is not working. What to do?
Try these steps: 1) Refresh the page 2) Clear browser cache 3) Try in incognito mode 4) Re-login 5) Check internet connection. Still not working? Email us at newstallyofficial@gmail.com
